Describe how search engines work

Search engines have two major functions: crawling and building an index, and providing search users with a ranked list of the websites they've determined are the most relevant. The best way for google to crawl is through links.These links will allow the search engines automated robots called crawlers or spiders to reach all the interconnect pages or documents on the web. Once the engines find these pages, they convert the code from them and store selected pieces in massive databases, to be recalled later when needed for a search query.

To accomplish the monumental task of holding billions of pages that can be accessed in a fraction of a second, the search engine companies have constructed datacenters all over the world.These monstrous storage facilities hold thousands of machines processing large quantities of information very quickly. When a person performs a search at any of the major engines, they demand results instantaneously; even a one- or two-second delay can cause dissatisfaction, so the engines work hard to provide answers as fast as possible.

Explain the importance of keywords and descriptions as meta tags

keywords and description as meta tags is very important for search engine optimization (SEO) listings. the keyword meta tag is very important because the key word you use is what the SEO will present to the users. The key words will determine your sites position on the search engine results page (SERP). Good keyword usage will take your page to the top of the SERPs page. That is, when a user do a search, a good keyword usage on your page will put your page at the top of the listing.

The description meta tag is vital as it is where you put your sites summary. What you offer. This is very important as google might use it as a snippet for your page if it does not find a good selection of text in your page content to use as snippet.

What constitutes good heading hierarchy and include an example with more than one heading tag

A good heading hierarchy make it easy for users and machines to understand the content. That is, it provides visual consistency. To implement a good heading hierarchy, it is importannt to note that there are 6 types of heading.

  • h1 should always be used at the top of the sequence and h6 at the bottom of the sequence.
  • Only one h1 element should be used on each page and at the top of the page content
  • Most headings on a page should be h2 or h3
  • h4 to h6 should be used only when neccessary especially on adding less important information on a page like a sidebar
  • And the headings should be short, unique, descriptive and on topic
